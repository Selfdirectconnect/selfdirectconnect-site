For Support Brokers

SelfDirectConnect is designed to give Support Brokers a professional space to build visibility and credibility within a structured, moderated platform.

The current challenge

Broker referrals often depend on informal networks, scattered lists, and word of mouth. That limits visibility, creates inconsistency, and makes it harder for families to compare options clearly. SelfDirectConnect is built to organize this ecosystem into one centralized professional platform.

What SelfDirectConnect provides

Professional visibility

A structured directory presence designed for families actively navigating Self Direction and searching for broker support.

Credibility through structure

Profiles emphasize professional information and clear service details so families can understand fit without confusion.

Opt in participation

Profiles go live only after the broker opts in. This is intentional and supports fairness, accuracy, and accountability.

Future premium features

As the platform grows, subscription tiers and premium profile options can support enhanced visibility and professional tools.

Trust and moderation

  • Profiles go live only after broker opt in
  • Reviews follow clear rules and a dispute process
  • Privacy and fairness are part of platform design
Early participation matters. During launch, SelfDirectConnect is onboarding interest and building the initial broker foundation before expanding visibility and features.
