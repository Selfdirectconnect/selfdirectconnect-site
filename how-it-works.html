How SelfDirectConnect works

SelfDirectConnect is a centralized resource and professional connection platform designed to simplify the Self Direction process for individuals with developmental disabilities and the families who support them.

Why this platform exists

The Self Direction system is often fragmented. Families are expected to piece together information across multiple sources, while Support Brokers and referral professionals rely on informal networks to connect. SelfDirectConnect organizes education and professional access in one place so people can move forward with clarity.

For families

Clear explanations, structured guidance, and practical resources so you can understand Self Direction and make confident decisions.

For Support Brokers

Professional visibility tools and a structured directory so families can understand your expertise and find the right fit.

For care managers and referral partners

A clean referral tool designed to replace scattered lists and word of mouth referrals with structured options.

For advertisers and partners

Brand safe opportunities to reach a focused audience of families and service professionals within the Self Direction ecosystem.

What we provide

  • Structured education on how Self Direction works, designed to reduce confusion and overwhelm
  • A searchable directory of Support Brokers built around professional profiles
  • A clear pathway for families to research, compare, and connect
  • A platform approach designed for trust, clarity, and responsible growth

How the platform works in practice

  • Families use educational guides to understand the process and what to look for
  • Support Brokers opt in to create and maintain a professional presence
  • Families review broker profiles and make contact based on fit, needs, and location
  • Care managers reference structured options rather than informal lists

Trust and moderation are part of the platform design. Profiles go live only after broker opt in. Reviews follow clear rules and a dispute process.

Launch approach

SelfDirectConnect is launching in phases. The initial focus is building a clean, simple structure, validating engagement, and onboarding early Support Broker profiles. As adoption grows, additional tools and features will be introduced based on community needs and platform performance.
