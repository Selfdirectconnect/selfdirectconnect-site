For families navigating Self Direction

Self Direction provides flexibility and control, but the process can feel overwhelming without clear structure. SelfDirectConnect is designed to simplify that experience.

What families often face

  • Unclear explanations of how Self Direction works
  • Difficulty comparing Support Brokers
  • Overreliance on informal referrals and word of mouth
  • Uncertainty about what questions to ask

What SelfDirectConnect provides

  • Plain language guidance about the Self Direction model
  • Structured information about the role of Support Brokers
  • A centralized directory built around professional profiles
  • A moderated framework designed for fairness and clarity
Families should not have to navigate Self Direction through scattered information. The goal is clarity, structure, and informed choice.
