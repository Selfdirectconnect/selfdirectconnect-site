How it will work

SelfDirectConnect is being designed as a structured, moderated platform that helps families compare services and make informed decisions while protecting fairness and privacy.

  • Families will be able to find resources and understand what to look for
  • Reviews and ratings will follow clear rules and moderation standards
  • Brokers and providers will be invited to opt in before public listing

Moderation first

The platform will not publish broker profiles or feedback without an opt in process and moderation safeguards. This is intentional to reduce misinformation, defamation risk, and privacy issues.
