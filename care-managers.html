For Care Managers and Referral Partners

SelfDirectConnect provides a structured tool that supports more consistent and transparent referrals within the Self Direction ecosystem.

The referral challenge

Many referrals rely on informal lists, personal contacts, or word of mouth. While relationships matter, families benefit from a centralized, structured resource that allows for clearer comparison and documentation.

What SelfDirectConnect offers professionals

  • A centralized directory of opt in Support Brokers
  • Structured profile information designed for transparency
  • A moderated environment that prioritizes fairness and accuracy
  • A cleaner way to guide families through the research process

Why this matters

A structured referral ecosystem reduces confusion, supports informed decision making, and strengthens trust across families, brokers, and professionals.

SelfDirectConnect is designed as infrastructure, not competition. The goal is to strengthen the ecosystem by organizing it.
