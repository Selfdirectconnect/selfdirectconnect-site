Participation Standards

SelfDirectConnect is being built to strengthen clarity, accountability, and coordination within the self direction ecosystem.

Participation on this platform follows defined standards designed to protect families, professionals, and the integrity of the system.

1. Opt In Participation

No professional profile will be published without explicit consent. Participation is voluntary and may be withdrawn at any time, subject to active engagement policies.

2. Verification and Accuracy

Participants are responsible for ensuring that all submitted information is accurate and current.

  • Professional credentials
  • Service scope
  • Areas of expertise
  • Organizational affiliation

Misrepresentation may result in profile suspension or removal.

3. Structured Profile Framework

Profiles follow a standardized format to ensure clarity and fairness. They are structured representations, not marketing pages.

4. Moderated Feedback Framework (Future Phase)

A structured feedback system is planned for a future phase. When implemented, it will:

  • Follow defined submission guidelines
  • Prohibit defamatory or unverified claims
  • Include a dispute and review process
  • Allow contextual response where appropriate

SelfDirectConnect reserves the right to moderate, delay, or decline publication of submissions.

5. Professional Conduct Expectations

Participants agree to:

  • Maintain respectful communication
  • Avoid misleading service representations
  • Engage in good faith
  • Follow platform participation rules

6. Platform Integrity

SelfDirectConnect may update participation standards as the platform evolves. The platform reserves the right to suspend participation, remove inaccurate content, and enforce moderation policies.

Our Vision

Self direction continues to grow. As it grows, structure becomes essential.

Participation on SelfDirectConnect is not simply about visibility. It is about contributing to a more organized, accountable, and coordinated ecosystem.
