Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Overview

SelfDirectConnect is a centralized resource and professional connection platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Information you submit through signup forms, such as your email address and selected role
  • Messages you send to us by email
  • Basic site analytics that help us understand site traffic and performance

How we use information

  • To send updates related to your selected role and platform launch progress
  • To respond to questions and support requests
  • To improve site performance, usability, and content organization
  • To maintain platform safety, moderation standards, and integrity

Email subscriptions and MailerLite

We use MailerLite to manage interest lists and send updates. When you submit a form, your information is processed by MailerLite according to their services and privacy practices.

You can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link included in our emails.

Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information only when necessary to operate the platform, comply with legal requirements, or protect safety and integrity.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed to operate the platform, provide updates you requested, comply with legal obligations, and maintain records required for safety and moderation.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information submitted through the site. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s privacy

SelfDirectConnect is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: selfdirectconnect@outlook.com
